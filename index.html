Escape the Island

Welcome to Escape the Island!

You are stranded on a deserted island and must find a way to escape. Your only hope is to follow the clues that lead to a hidden treasure, which contains a ship that can help you leave the island.

In this game, you have to answer 5 quizzes which has a one word answer

If you entered the correct answer in the box then you'll entered into the next level

If your answer is wrong then you'll get two chances to re-attempt it

To start the game, scroll down and click on 'Start the Game' button.
